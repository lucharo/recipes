Ingredients⠀  
2 cans Garbanzo beans (Chickpeas) (2x 15 oz)⠀  
7 tbsp Aquafaba (drained liquid from the canned chickpeas)⠀  
6 tbsp Lemon Juice (1/4 cup + 2 tbsp)⠀  
1/4 cup Tahini⠀  
2 tsp Cumin⠀  
2 tsp Garlic powder⠀  
1 1/2 tsp Salt⠀  
.⠀  
Instructions⠀  
1. Drain the liquid from the garbanzo beans but keep it in a jar or bowl. ⠀  
2. Rinse the chickpeas well and transfer to a food processor.⠀  
3. Add the remaining ingredients to the food processor, including 7 tbsps from the reserved aquafaba form step 1. ⠀  
4. Process until as smooth as possible. Adjust the seasonings. ⠀  
5. Refrigerate 1 hour before serving. (Otherwise the hummus is room temperature. It tastes better when it’s a bit cold.)⠀  
6. Keep refrigerated in a sealed container for up to 1 week. ⠀
