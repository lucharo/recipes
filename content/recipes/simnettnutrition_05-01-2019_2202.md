1 tbsp tamari  
1 tsp of hot sauce (or more)  
1 tsp garlic powder  
1 tsp of onion powder  
1 tsp grainy mustard  
1 tsp of maple syrup (optional)  
  
Mix all these together and marinate tempeh overnight or press it into tempeh with your fingers (or back of a spoon) if you do not want to marinate it overnight. Bake at 375 F for 10-12 minutes.
