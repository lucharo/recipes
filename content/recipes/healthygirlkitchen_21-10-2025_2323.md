You need:   
2 cups almond milk   
2 cups organic rolled oats   
2 mashed bananas   
2 tsp vanilla   
1 tsp baking powder   
2 tbsp ground flax   
2 tbsp pure maple syrup   
1/2 cup chopped walnuts   
1 cup wild blueberries (I used frozen) you can use any kind of blueberries  
1 cup chopped strawberries   
1 tsp cinnamon   
  
1. Preheat oven to 350.  
2. In a 9x9 baking dish - mix all wet ingredients together.   
3. Add the dry ingredients and mix.   
4. Add in berries and cinnamon and mix again.  Line the top with thin banana slices if you want (I used 2 extra bananas for this). And drizzle with maple syrup.   
5. Bake for 30 min at 350 F.   
6. Enjoy! Best eaten warm out of the oven but you can reheat for up to 3 days. Add a splash of milk when reheating then drizzle w a little maple syrup.
