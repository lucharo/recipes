what you’ll need:  
BECHAMEL:  
- 70ml water  
- 3 chopped cloves of garlic  
- 1 chopped onion  
- 75g flour  
- 700ml soy milk  
- 2tbsp lemon juice  
- pepper  
- nutmeg  
  
TOMATO SAUCE:  
- 1 chopped garlic clove  
- italian herbs  
- 100g corn  
- 500ml tomato sauce  
  
SPINACH LAYER:  
- 1 chopped stalk of leek  
- 200g frozen peas  
- 350g frozen spinach  
- italian herbs  
- pepper - 50g breadcrumbs  
- some seeds
