Teriyaki Mushrooms  
2 shiitake mushrooms (rehydrated, see notes) or other mushrooms of choice  
1-2 small king oyster mushrooms, thinly sliced  
Neutral oil or vegan butter  
1 tbsp mirin  
1 tbsp sake  
1 tbsp soy sauce  
1 tsp dark soy sauce, optional for colour  
1.5 tsp sugar, or more to taste  
  
Broth  
1 tbsp vegan butter or oil  
2 cloves garlic, minced  
1 tbsp Asian sesame paste or nut butter  
2 cups mushroom broth from soaked mushrooms  
2 cups @lechia.co unsweetened macadamia nut milk  
1 tbsp miso paste, I used white miso  
2 tbsp soy sauce  
1 tbsp sake or mirin (optional)  
Salt, to taste  
  
Noodles and Serving  
2 servings udon noodles (I simply microwaved frozen udon until chewy)  
Layu or Japanese chili oil  
Sesame oil  
Spring onions or green onions, for topping  
Sesame seeds
