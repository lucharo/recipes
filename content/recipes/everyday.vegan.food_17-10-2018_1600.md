Dark chocolate truffles: .  
Ingredients:⠀  
1/4 cup almond milk (2 oz)⠀  
100g/3.5 oz 78% cacao dark chocolate (1 chocolate bar) ⠀  
1 tbsp (5g) cacao powder⠀  
.⠀  
Instructions:⠀  
1. Heat the almond in a small saucepan over medium heat until just starting to simmer.⠀  
2. Roughly chop the chocolate and set aside.⠀  
3. Immediately add the chocolate pieces to the bubbling milk, turn off the heat and cover with a lid. Do not touch for 10 minutes to allow the chocolate to soften. ⠀  
4. Then lift cover and use a whisk to whisk until smooth. Whisk until completely melted, creamy, and smooth. Even if it doesn’t seem like it will become smooth, keep whisking.⠀  
5. Set the mixture in the refrigerator to chill uncovered for 1-2 hours, or until almost completely solid. ⠀  
6. Once chilled and firm, prepare a small dish of cocoa or cacao powder for rolling (optional).⠀  
7. Use a tablespoon to scoop out 5 small balls, then use your hands to gently but quickly roll/form the chocolate into balls. Place the rolled balls back in the refrigerator or freezer for 5 minutes.⠀  
8. Take out of the refrigerator/freezer and roll again to be make them rounder. They are a bit firmer at this point.⠀  
9. Toss in cacao powder to coat and shake off excess.⠀  
10. Enjoy truffles immediately, or refrigerate a bit to firm up.⠀  
11. Store truffles covered in the refrigerator for up to 5-7 days.⠀
