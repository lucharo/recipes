🌮🌱 Crispy Baked Taquitos   
  
🌱 By @that.veganbabe  
  
With an avocado cilantro lime dip 🤤 🥑   
  
I topped these bad boys with my healthy homemade vegan cheese sauce & salsa - full written recipe can be found on my website!! 🙌🎉  
  
✨INGREDIENTS✨  
12 mini tortillas  
1/2 onion  
3 cloves garlic  
1 small green pepper  
1 small orange pepper  
400g extra firm pressed tofu  
1/2 cup fire roasted tomatoes (or tomato sauce)  
3 tbsp taco seasoning   
Handful of cilantro  
Juice of 1 lime  
1/2 cup raw cashews  
1/4 cup water  
  
🥑 Avocado Cilantro Lime Dip  
1 avocado  
1 jalapeño, deseeded  
Handful cilantro  
1/4 cup hemp seeds  
Juice of 1-2 limes  
1 clove garlic  
1 tsp maple syrup  
3-4 tbsp water  
  
✅ Sauté your onion until translucent then add in your garlic & bell peppers.  
  
😋 Shred your tofu & add it into your veggies along with some taco seasoning & fire roasted tomatoes.  
  
🤙Make a cashew cream by blending together your cashews & water. If you do not have a high speed blender make sure to soak your cashews first!   
  
🥄 Mix in your cashew cream & add in some chopped cilantro & fresh lime juice.  
  
🤗 Add the filling to mini tortillas and roll them up nice and tight!  
  
🔥 Add your taquitos to a greased baking dish, brush with some olive oil and bake at 400f for 25-30 minutes until golden.  
  
🤤 Serve & enjoy!
