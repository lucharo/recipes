This might be my new favourite dessert! The combination of these ingredients is simply.. sensational. It took me by the hand and whispered sweet nothings in my ear 🤤  
   
INGREDIENTS  
   
600g sweet potatoes  
8 pitted dates  
400ml organic coconut milk  
1/2 fresh nutmeg, grated  
1 cube of ginger  
1 tbsp cinnamon  
1 tbsp vanilla extract  
   
1.   Preheat your oven to 200°C.  
2.   Place sweet potatoes in a baking dish, light rub with oil and pierce a few times with a fork.  
3.   Roast in the oven for approx 30 minutes until soft.  
4.   Once the sweet potatoes have cooled, add it to a blender along with the other ingredients listed.  
5.   Blend until smooth, then pour the mixture into the Ninja Creami tub.  
6.   Freeze for 24 hours.  
7.   Let the Creami do the rest!  
   
I served each scoop of ice cream on top of fried plantain cups which I cooked and squeezed in a lemon squeezer to form cup shapes and topped with chopped pecans that I toasted off in a pan with butter and a little cinnamon.
