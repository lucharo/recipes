BRAZILIAN LEMONADE AKA SWISS LEMONADE - a creamy, refreshing Brazilian drink made with only 3 ingredients: limes, sweetened condensed milk and water, it’s so gooood  
  
Ingredients (makes 4 to 6 servings)  
  
5 limes, washed and quartered   
1 quart water  
1 x 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk   
1 quart ice  
  
Instructions   
  
1. Blend the water and limes together, pulsing until roughly blended. Don’t blend for longer than 1 minute, the lemonade will turn bitter (due to breaking down the bitter pith).   
  
2. Strain the blended lime juice using a mesh strainer, in a serving container. The mesh strainer will catch all the blended lime bits.   
  
3. Rinse the blender. Add the lime water back to the blender. Add the condensed milk and ice. Blend until smooth and frothy. The mixture with turn light in color. Taste and add more ice or water as needed.   
  
4. Serve right away in chilled glasses.
