Tomato Soup:  
1x tin of cherry tomatoes  
1 x tin of full-fat coconut milk  
1 onion, diced  
2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced  
1 tsp smoked paprika  
1 tsp dried oregano  
2 tsp olive oil  
Pinch of salt and black pepper  
  
Grilled Cheese:  
Sourdough bread  
Plant-based butter  
Vegan cheese
