Teamed up with @adonisfoods to create this DELICIOUS breakfast! Absolutely packed with protein (and made using their peanut butter and chocolate high protein bar!) ✨ highly recommend these just as a snack on the go too! Use my code LIV15 for money off your purchase! 🥰  
  
CHOCOLATE PEANUT BUTTER OATS WITH NUTTY CRUST 🍫  
  
50g instant oats  
30g vegan chocolate protein powder   
Tbsp cocoa powder  
1/2tsp baking powder   
140ml almond milk  
Sweetener of choice to taste  
  
For the nutty ‘crust’:  
- 1/2 an @adonis high protein peanut & chocolate bar (or sub for peanuts)  
- 1tsp yoghurt  
- Small splash of almond milk if needed  
  
To fill:  
- square of dark chocolate  
- Tbsp peanut butter  
  
🥣 mix the oat ingredients together and 3/4 pop in a bowl  
🥣 Place the peanut butter and chocolate on top, pour the remaining little bit of mixture on top, leaving room for the crust   
🥣 Now mix together your nutty ‘crust’ ingredients till combined   
🥣 then place this mixture over the top of the oats/ chocolate and peanut butter   
🥣 bake in the oven for 20 mins at 170
