This dish has got everything you could want; creamy, spicy goodness with noodles, protein + fresh veggies 😯   
  
Super simple recipe and you can use up any veggies you may have in your fridge, enjoy + get creative with this one!  
______________________________________  
  
⭐️ INGREDIENTS ⭐️   
  
-ramen noodles to serve 2  
-1/4 inch minced ginger  
-3 garlic cloves  
-1/2 tsp chilli flakes  
-1/2 tbsp miso paste  
-2 tbsp tahini  
-1/2 tbsp mirin  
-1/2 cup vegan milk (oat or soy works best)  
-1 cup water  
-spring onion  
-sesame seeds  
-firm tofu (baked or fried till crispy)  
-beansprouts  
  
⭐️ METHOD ⭐️  
  
-start by frying the garlic, ginger + chilli flakes on a low/medium heat for around 5 minutes.  
-add the miso paste, tahini, mirin + soy sauce then combine into a paste.  
-add the water + oat milk then simmer on a low heat for another 5 minutes, I then strained the broth to make it smoother but you don’t have to.  
-place some cooked noodles in a bowl then pour over the broth then add the cooked tofu, sweetcorn, beans sprouts + spring onion.  
-top with sesame seeds, chilli oil + sesame oil then serve + enjoy 😊
