In this week’s episode we’re checking out Tofu Shoten in Brunswick, Melbourne. They do tofu in every shape and form here, from tofu donuts to tofu fritters, fresh soy milk, fresh tofu for cooking, soy puddings and more. Be sure to save this for your next trip to Melbourne!   
  
If you love tofu, you’re in the right spot. This is a series where I hunt down some of the best, weirdest, and most wonderful tofu and soy creations around. From crispy salt & pepper to wobbly soft serve, we’re celebrating tofu in all its glory!  
  
THE SPOT:   
📍Tofu Shoten, Bruswick (Melbourne) @tofushoten   
  
DIETARIES:  
🥬  LOTS of vegan-friendly options! Most things in-store are vegan.
