1 cup sticky rice, soaked overnight  
200ml coconut cream   
1-2 tbsp sugar  
pinch of salt  
fresh mango   
  
Steam rice for 20-25 minutes. Whilst steaming, mix sugar and salt into coconut cream and heat on low til sugar dissolves. Reserve about half the coconut cream for pouring in a small cup. Slice your mango. Once the rice is finished steaming, mix into the coconut cream and cook on low heat, stirring until the coconut cream is absorbed into the rice. Serve hot with fresh mango and sprinkle with toasted sesame or mung beans.
