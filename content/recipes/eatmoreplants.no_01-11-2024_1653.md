Ingredients (4 servings) 1 small head cauliflower 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup almond milk 1 tsp garlic powder 0.5 tsp salt 1 cup breadcrumbs  
  
Gochujang Glaze: 1 garlic clove, minced 1 tsp fresh grated ginger 0.25 cup soy sauce 1 tbsp maple syrup or sweetener of choice 1 tbsp sesame oil 1 tbsp rice vinegar 2 tbsp gochujang paste 1 tbsp sriracha  
  
Topping: 1 tsp sesame seeds 2 tbsp green onions  
  
Instructions: 1. Cut the cauliflower into medium florets. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, milk, garlic powder, and salt  
2.  Add the cauliflower to the batter and toss to coat. Now toss the coated cauliflower florets in the breadcrumbs and mix until well coated  
3.  Air-fry on 200°C for 10-15 minutes, or until crispy.  
4. Now add all the ingredients for the gochujang glaze to a blender and blend until smooth.  
5.  When the cauliflower is done, add it to a bowl and toss with the glaze. Sprinkle on sesame seeds and chopped scallions and enjoy!
