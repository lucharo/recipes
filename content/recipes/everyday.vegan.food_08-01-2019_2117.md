1 3/4 cup water  
1 can of fire roasted diced tomatoes with garlic (411g/14.5oz)  
1 tbsp nutritional yeast  
1 tsp onion powder  
1/2 tsp salt  
1/4 tsp pepper  
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes  
1/8 tsp cayenne  
1 cup short grain brown rice (190g)  
  
Instructions:  
1. Start boiling water. Add all of the seasonings and mix well. Add the rice and mix.  
2. Simmer with the lid on for 45 minutes, turn off the heat and let rest 15 minutes.  
  
Serves 4 portions.   
Yellow Jasmine Rice  
  
1 1/2 cup water  
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder  
1 1/2 tsp onion powder  
1 tsp sumac  
1/2 tsp cumin  
1/2 tsp salt  
1/4 tsp pepper  
1/8 tsp turmeric  
1 cup jasmine rice (195g)  
  
Instructions:  
1. Bring the water to a boil. Add the seasonings and mix.  
2. Add the rice, mix, place a lid on it and simmer for 15 minutes. Turn off the heat and let rest 15 minutes.  
  
Serves 3 portions.  
  
Roasted Cauliflower  
  
1 medium head cauliflower  
1 tbsp oil  
1 tbsp  
1 tbsp nutritional yeast  
1 tbsp lemon zest  
1 tsp maple syrup  
1 tsp balsamic vinegar  
1 1/2 tsp garlic powder  
1 tsp onion powder  
1/2 to 3/4 tsp salt  
1/2 tsp pepper  
  
Instructions  
1. Preheat oven to 450F. Brush a large or 2 med baking sheet(s), with oil or use parchment paper.  
2. Combine the remaining ingredients. It will look like a paste, that’s normal.  
3. Chop the cauliflower into bite size pieces to medium pieces.  
4. Add the chopped cauliflower to the bowl, and mix with your hands, making sure the cauliflower is well coated with the paste.  
5. Place the coated cauliflower pieces on the prepared baking sheet, flat side down to get a lot of roasting/browning.  
6. Bake for a total of 18-20 minutes or less. Flip the cauliflower after 13 minutes.  
7. Serve immediately.   
Serves 6.

1  tsp dill   
1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:  
1. Combine the marinade ingredients in a medium bowl and mix.  
2. Heat a skillet on medium-high heat. Cook the tofu with the marinade in the skillet. Mix often.
