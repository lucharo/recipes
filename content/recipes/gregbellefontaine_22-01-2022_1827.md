Here’s what I used:

- splash @silk_canada cashew milk  
- 1/2 cup @justegg from @luminate_co   
-  1 tsp cinnamon   
- 2 tbsp @becelca vegan butter  
- 1/4 cup crushed pistachios   
- maple syrup!
