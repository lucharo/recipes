By @bakesby.britt   
  
Recipe:  
  
Ingredients:  
40 gr oats  
150 ml plant milk  
50 ml coffee (or dairy-free chocolate milk)  
  
Topping:  
vegan yogurt  
dark chocolate  
cocoa powder  
  
Mix all ingredients for the oatmeal together.  Heat it up in a pan for about 4 minutes.  
  
Top with toppings.  
  
Enjoy!
