This is a great no-cook recipe perfect for those busy and hot days (esp in this Philippine heat 🥵) since it can be incredibly refreshing yet hearty at the same time + it’s ready in 10 mins or less!  
  
I topped mine with vegan “pork” laver floss (store-bought), spring onions, and sesame seeds but you can opt to add any other toppings you’d like!  
  
THE SAUCE  
- hoisin sauce  
- garlic, minced  
- soy sauce, to taste  
- dark soy sauce, optional for colour  
- sugar  
- rice vinegar  
- sesame oil  
- chili garlic sauce or chili oil of choice  
  
👉🏼you can check out the detailed recipe in my website www.thefoodietakesflight.com!  
  
It’s a pretty loose one and you can use the measurements as a guide and just adjust everything to your taste. It’s also linked in my bio @thefoodietakesflight 🌞
