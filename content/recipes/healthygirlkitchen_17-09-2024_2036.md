1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed   
1/2 cup peanut butter   
1/2 cup maple syrup   
1/2 cup Wellious x HealthyGirl chocolate PB protein   
1/4 cup cocoa   
2 tbsp oil   
1 tsp vanilla   
Pinch salt   
1/4 tsp baking soda   
1/4 tsp baking powder  
1/2 cup dark chocolate chips for topping   
  
1. Preheat oven to 350 F.   
2. Add all ingredients to food processor.  
3. Blend until smooth.  
4. Line 8x8 brownie pan with parchment or grease w oil. Evenly spread the batter in the pan. Top with chocolate chips. Bake for 25 minutes!   
5. Let cool for 30, top with flaky salt and enjoy!!!
