title: "TESTING A DILL-PICKLE COCKTAIL for a Pickle Party! 🥒 I was searching for something a little more interesting than a Dill Pickle Martini to serve as a drink. I stumbled across on a recipe for a Dill Pickle Tom Collins, a drink usually made from gin, lemon juice, sugar and soda water. The Dill Pickle version recipe calls for a base of Dill Aquavit, a scandi spirit commonly infused with herbs like dill and fennel, but I wasn’t able to source any and won’t be able to get it delivered in time (boooo) so I opted for gin. Instead, I infused my simple syrup with dill and caraway to really try to amp up those pickle flavours and oh my my my the syrup turned out fab. Overall the drink is extremely refreshing, nice and salty and bubbly - it’s a real delight. I’ve make some tweaks to the original recipe and willl make a few more before serving. I LOVE THIS COMBO."
