CRISPY VIETNAMESE PANCAKES (BÁNH XÈO) with @hexclad_aus 🇻🇳 One of my all-time favourite dishes that is also perfect for entertaining. The nonstick hybrid tech on these bad boys makes it oh-so-easy to get these pancakes to the perfect level of glorious crispiness. I went for a Central Vietnamese style pancake today - smaller crispier and the little 8-inch pans are ideal for this SO CUTE 🥹  
  
BATTER  
125g rice flour (use a very fine rice flour)  
45g all purpose flour (substitute for cornstarch if GF)  
1 tsp turmeric  
1 3/4 cup water  
200ml coconut cream (I used Kara)  
1/2 tsp salt  
1 spring onion, diced  
  
FILLING  
Blanched Bean Sprouts  
King Oyster Mushrooms  
  
TO SERVE  
Lettuce  
Herbs (I suggest mint, Vietnamese coriander and shiso)  
Vegan Vietnamese Dipping Sauce  
Đồ Chua
