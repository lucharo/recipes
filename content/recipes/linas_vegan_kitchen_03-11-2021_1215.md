🚨🚨SAVE RECIPE FOR LATER🚨🚨  
  
RECIPE:  
Ingredients:  
1 cup all purpose flour  
1 tbsp baking powder  
1 tsp baking soda  
3/4 cup granulated sugar  
1/4 cup cocoa powder   
1 tsp salt  
4 ripe bananas  
1 cup almond milk (sub other plant milk)  
1/4 cup neutral oil (I used vegetable)  
1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips (add more or less according to liking)  
  
Directions:  
  
1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit and grease a loaf tin.  
2. In a large bowl, mash 4 ripe bananas and combine with 1 cup flour, 1 tbsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 3/4 cup sugar, 1/4 cup cocoa powder and 1 tsp salt.  
3. Stir in 1 cup almond milk and 1/4 cup oil.   
4. Once well combined, add in vegan chocolate chips according to liking.  
5. Pour batter into greased loaf tin and bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. (Sometimes it takes a bit longer than this but trust me it’s worth the wait!!)  
6. Remove from oven and allow to cool for at least 5 minutes. Enjoy!!
