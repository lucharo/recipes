So I used @hellolingo to track my glucose in real time. Lingo shows how my body responds to food, stress, sleep, and more - and I had to put my date sweetened banana bread to the test 🍌  
  
The results? I was pleasantly surprised 😅 Smooth sailing for my glucose!  
Now I’m even more obsessed with baking in a way that supports steady energy.  
  
Recipe:  
300g flour  
300g overripe bananas  
90g dates, seeds removed  
30g (1/4 cup) ground flax seeds +120ml warm water  
120ml olive oil  
1 tbsp lemon juice  
1 tsp baking soda  
1/2 tsp baking powder  
1/4 tsp salt  
100g dark chocolate chips (optional)
