Soak in some warm water 15 dates (best if you can use some medjool dates) then add them to a food processor with 1/2 cup of ground almonds, the zest of 1/2 unwaxed orange, 1tbsp of almond butter and 3-4 tbsp of raw cacao powder. Blend everything until you have a sticky paste. Roll the mixture into balls and coat them in some extra cacao powder. Place them in true fridge to set for 1 hour. At this point you can either eat them as they are or coat them into some melted dark chocolate to make them extra indulgent 🤤( I have used @miaflavour 75% dark 👌🏻) You can store them in the freezer so they will last several weeks if you manage not to eat them all in one go as I did 😆 #makeyourskinhappy
