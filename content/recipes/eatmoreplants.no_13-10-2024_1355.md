Ingredients (3-4 servings)  
Tofu balls:  
1 block of extra firm Tofu  
1 tbsp minced chopped Ginger  
1 tbsp minced chopped red Chili  
2 minced garlic cloves   
1 tsp Lime zest   
1 tsp onion powder  
1 tsp salt  
4-6 tbsp Chickpea flour (gluten free) or cornstarch   
  
Sauce:  
1 tbsp minced Ginger  
2 monced garlic cloves  
2 tbsp fresh coriander  
1 tsp finley chopped red Chili  
2 tbsp Red curry paste  
2 cups (5 dl) Oyster mushrooms  
2 carrots  
1 can Coconut milk  
1 tbsp vegetable broth powder  
2 cups water   
  
Assemble:  
broccoli   
red rice  
  
Instructions:  
1. Start by making the tofu balls. Scramble your tofu into a bowl, using your hands. Add the rest of the ingredients listed under «tofu balls». Knead for a few minutes and form into balls. Add more flour if needed.  
2. Cook your rice according to package instructions.  
3. Fry your tofu balls in a non stick pan, or pop them in the oven, or an air fryer until golden and crisp on the outside. You can also add the broccoli into the oven/air fryer to cook it, or simply steam or fry it for a few minutes.  
4. Make the sauce by frying ginger, garlic, coriander, chili for a few minutes, add carrots (I use a peeler to get thin carrot spagetti strips) and chopped mushrooms. Fry for another 2 minutes before adding coconut milk vegetable broth powder and water.   
5. Assemble and enjoy!
