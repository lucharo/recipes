• FIBER. Chickpeas are packed with fiber 🌾 which helps your bowels get movin’ 🚗 and your microbiome groovin’ 💃. Hopefully you know by now that I’m OBSESSED with PREbiotic fiber and POSTbiotic short chain fatty acids. They’re game changers for both gut and total body health. Chick 🐣 peas are also loaded with protein, iron, and folate which help you feel full and energized. ⚡️  
.  
• HEALTHY FAT ALERT! 🚨 The cashew butter contains all kinds of good stuff, like zinc, which is anti-inflammatory/antioxidant and helps your immune system 😷 (extra important around this time of the year). In a randomized-controlled trial, cashews were found to lower total cholesterol and LDL cholesterol.  
.  
🍫 The dark chocolate is packed with antioxidants, and cocoa consumption is associated with reduced ❌ fat accumulation and may lower long term weight gain. That’s called #winning  
.  
RECIPE:   
1 can of chickpeas, rinsed   
¼ cup of cashew butter   
2 ½ tbs of maple syrup   
½ tsp of cinnamon   
1 tsp of vanilla   
Add all ingredients to a food processor or high-power blender. Blend until smooth, and then add dark chocolate chunks or cocao nibs.  
.
