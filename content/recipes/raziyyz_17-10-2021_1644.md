Ingredients:  
2 oz. Espresso Shot OR 1/4 cup Brewed Coffee (an espresso will give you a bolder flavour and a stronger kick)  
1/3 cup Rolled Oats (or Quick Oats)  
1/2 cup Fat Free Greek Yogurt  
1 tbsp Low Calorie Maple Syrup (or any sweetener of your choice)  
1/4 tsp Cinnamon  
1 scoop Protein Powder (I use a Vanilla flavour)  
-  
Toppings (Optional):  
Extra Cinnamon   
Granola  
Other Options may include any kind of Nuts, Seeds, Fruits, and Coconut Flakes  
-  
NOTES: If you choose to use Honey or Pure Maple Syrup, keep in mind the extra calories!  
-  
TIP: Prep these in batches for the week. It lasts for up to approx. 5 days in the fridge.  
-  
Serving Size: 1  
-  
CALORIES/MACROS (ENTIRE RECIPE):  
Approx. 314 Calories  
37g Protein  
35g Carbs  
3g Fat  
-  
Numbers may slightly vary depending on the ingredient brands you use!
