MUSHROOM STROGANOFF\
Cashew Cream\
1.5 cups roasted cashews, soaked in hot water then drained \
2 cups water\
2.5 tbsp beef stock powder\
1 tbsp dijon \
4 tbsp apple cider vinegar\

Other\
3 tbsp vegan butter\
800g botton mushrooms\
2 brown onions\
1 tbsp minced garlic\
1 tsp dried thyme\
3 tbsp tomato paste\
1/2 cup red wine \
1 tsp black pepper \
1 cup water\
