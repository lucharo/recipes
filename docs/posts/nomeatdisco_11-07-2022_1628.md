# STICKY AUBERGINE ASIAN RICE BOWL 🍆  

> recipe by [@nomeatdisco](https://www.instagram.com/nomeatdisco/) 
(Sam Jones | No Meat Disco) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/Cf4O_YXKdyu)

![](../img/nomeatdisco_11-07-2022_1628.png)

  
Aubergine is a very frowned upon veg to cook with as its quite hard to get right when cooking. I tend to steam mine first which makes it a real nice soft texture and then fry it off after and that’s exactly what me and Ben did with this dish. Created a sticky marinade to go with it and it was genuinely incredible!   
  
Ingredients  
1 Aubergine  
2 Cloves garlic  
1 Thumb Piece of Ginger  
1 Tbsp Sesame Oil  
1 Tbsp Soy Sauce  
1 Tbsp Dark Soy Sauce  
1 Tbsp Rice Wine Vinegar  
1 Tbsp Maple Syrup  
Sesame Seeds  
Vegetable Oil  
  
Rice  
Pak Choy  
Spring Onions  
  
Method:  
1. Start by steaming your aubergine for 10-15 mins.   
2. Whilst that’s steaming mix together your marinade ingredients in a jar.   
3. Once steamed slice it down the middle but not all the way and then criss cross score it on the flesh.   
4. Now add some veg oil to a pan and add your aubergine to the pan and fry for a few minutes each side.   
5. No drizzle over your marinade and fry the aubergine until sticky and golden.  
6. Now char off your pan choy and spring onions in some oil and soy sauce and serve the aubergine with rice and the veg and ENJOY!!
