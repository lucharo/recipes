# SOY & GARLIC STEAMED SILKEN TOFU 🍲  

> recipe by [@nomeatdisco](https://www.instagram.com/nomeatdisco/) 
(Sam Jones | No Meat Disco) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/ClLqWpSqQ4k)

![](../img/nomeatdisco_20-11-2022_1210.png)

  
Fancy a quickie is taking off nicely at the moment, giving you meals and ingredients that are very accessible and affordable.   
  
This meal took me less than 10 minutes and was an amazing lunchtime light meal and lovely and warming on these cold days!   
  
100% you need to give this a go 🤘😍  
  
Ingredients   
1 Block of silken tofu  
3 Tbsp Soy sauce  
1/2 Tbsp Rice Vinegar   
1 Tbsp Brown Sugar  
1 Tsp savory all purpose seasoning   
2 Cloves garlic  
1 Thumb piece of ginger  
1 Tsp Chilli Oil  
1 Tsp crispy Chilli oil for the top  
  
Serve with rice your choice of veg  
Pickled ginger and sesame seeds and spring onions  
  
Method:  
1. Boil some water and steam your tofu and veg for 8 mins.  
2. Put your rice on the boil and cook to packet instructions.  
3. Now mix together the sauce ingredients and shake well.  
4. Once tofu is steamed, drain the water and pour over the sauce ingredients.  
5. Serve up the rice and top with the silken tofu and more of that sauce and the veg and sesame seeds and enjoy!
