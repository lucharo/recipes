# Tofu Chick’n Teriyaki made extra ‘meaty’ by freezing blocks of tofu! 🥢 

> recipe by [@thefoodietakesflight](https://www.instagram.com/thefoodietakesflight/) 
(Jeeca • Vegan Recipes 🥢) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/CjYCIIGJkfM)

![](../img/thefoodietakesflight_06-10-2022_1424.png)

  
Have you ever tried freezing tofu? I sliced blocks of tofu into 1” thick slabs (without pressing) before freezing these overnight.  
  
When freezing tofu, the water in the tofu expands as it freezes and creates these amazing layers in the tofu—giving the pieces that really “meaty” texture.  
  
I then thaw the tofu, squeeze out the excess liquid and then season the slabs with salt & pepper before coating in some flour & cooking down in a homemade teriyaki sauce 👌🏻
