# Sticky Pecan Banana Cinnabuns 🍌 The yeasted banana bread is soooo soft and delicious. Rough measurements below! Will retest and type up a full recipe soon 🫶🏼 

> recipe by [@itslizmiu](https://www.instagram.com/itslizmiu/) 
(Liz Miu 苗可玉 🍐) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/CiM86_TA9YN)

![](../img/itslizmiu_07-09-2022_1039.png)

  
DOUGH  
2.5 tsp instant yeast  
1 cup oat milk  
1/2 tsp salt   
1/4 cup oil  
65g cup sugar  
360g ripe mashed bananas  
750g AP flour   
  
FILLING   
170g vegan butter  
170g brown sugar  
2 tbsp cinnamon   
1tbsp cocoa powder  
-  
1 banana  
50g chopped pecans  
  
SAUCE  
80g vegan butter  
65g coconut cream  
200g brown sugar  
Pinch of salt   
100g chopped pecans
