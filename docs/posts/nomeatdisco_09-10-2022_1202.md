# VEGAN DUCK PANCAKES WITH MUSHROOMS 🍄 🦆  

> recipe by [@nomeatdisco](https://www.instagram.com/nomeatdisco/) 
(Sam Jones | No Meat Disco) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/CjffgFXpGeJ)

![](../img/nomeatdisco_09-10-2022_1202.png)

  
I mean life doesn’t get much better than duck pancakes does it? It’s pretty special, this literally took me about 10-15 mins to make! Super super simple! 😍  
  
Ingredients   
1 Punnet oyster mushrooms  
1/2 Cup Corn Starch   
1 Tbsp Chinese five spice   
2 Tbsp Hoisin Sauce  
Half a cucumber   
2 Spring Onions  
Duck pancakes  
Veg oil  
  
Method:  
1. Start by tearing your oyster mushrooms into strips.   
2. Add them to a bowl and cover them in Chinese five spice and then corn starch and make sure they’re all coated.  
3. Heat up the veg oil until frying point and batch cook your mushroom strips.  
4. Add them back to a bowl with the hoisin sauce and mix together well until coated.  
5. Now cut up your cucumber and spring onions into strips, load them on your duck pancakes along with the mushrooms and drizzle in more hoisin sauce.
