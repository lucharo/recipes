# Strawberry Overnight Oats 🍓✨Comment “overnight oats” below and I’ll message you the recipe 💌 

> recipe by [@frommybowl](https://www.instagram.com/frommybowl/) 
(From My Bowl | by Caitlin S) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/DH80SqjRvNX)

![](../img/frommybowl_02-04-2025_1606.png)

  
These creamy strawberry overnight oats are PACKED with berry flavor thanks to their gorgeous “strawberry milk”, ensuring you get a bit of strawberry in every single bite!   
  
This recipe is naturally sweetened and full of healthy fats + fiber to keep you full until lunch. If you want to add more protein to this, add a scoop of your favorite vanilla protein powder to the “milk” ingredients along with 1/4 cup extra milk and omit the sweetener.  
  
🍓 STRAWBERRY OVERNIGHT OATS 🍓  
➖For the Strawberry Milk:   
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberry halves  
1 3/4 cup creamy non-dairy milk  
1/4 cup unsweetened vegan yogurt (or replace with more milk)  
2 tablespoons grade A maple syrup, plus more to taste  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
Juice of 1/2 small lemon   
Pinch of salt  
➖For the Oatmeal:   
2 cups quick-cooking or rolled oats (I like a 50-50 mix)  
2 tablespoons chia seeds or ground flaxseed  
3/4 cup diced strawberries  
Topping suggestions: nut butter hemp hearts, almonds, etc.
