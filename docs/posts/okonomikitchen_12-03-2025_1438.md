# Sometimes you need to eat a head of cabbage, this is one of my favourite ways to do it: yamitsuki cabbage !! 🥬  

> recipe by [@okonomikitchen](https://www.instagram.com/okonomikitchen/) 
(Lisa Kitahara) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/DHGlVzwAvtM)

![](../img/okonomikitchen_12-03-2025_1438.png)

  
Yamitsuki means addictive in Japanese and it truly lives up to its name. Recommended to have at izakaya and popular for home cooks because it takes 5 minutes to make! You can use other vegetables like cucumber, lettuce, broccoli or carrots too.   
  
Key component is umami seasonings, I used shio koji, shio kombu and msg but there are lots of options to choose from :—)   
  
Full recipe on okonomikitchen blog ~
