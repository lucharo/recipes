# In Episode Two of Flavours of the Arab Golden Age, an Egyptian historian, Kotoz Ahmed, and a Palestinian chef, Fadi Kattan, discover sweets in Spain that are a legacy of Muslims of al Andalus. After the expulsion of the Muslims in the 15th century, a few remained and preserved their Andalusian cuisine, like bunuelos, a type of Spanish fritter.
