# Turmeric Ginger Congee 💛 This one is cozy, flavorful and amazing for batch cooking. It’s flavored with a spiced oil, ginger and turmeric and finished with crispy mushrooms and tofu to make it a balanced meal. I opted to make mine in the instant pot but this can also be made on the stove! 

> recipe by [@frommybowl](https://www.instagram.com/frommybowl/) 
(From My Bowl | by Caitlin S) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/DSkyy_BEcMO)

![](../img/frommybowl_22-12-2025_1756.png)

  
For the Congee:  
3/4 cup white jasmine rice  
1 teaspoon turmeric  
2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger peeled  
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt plus more to taste  
6 cups water  
  
For the Toppings:  
7-8 ounces mushrooms sliced (I like to use a mix)  
kosher salt to taste  
2 1/2 tablespoons avocado oil divided  
16 ounces super firm tofu cubed  
1 teaspoon garlic powder  
  
For the Tadka:  
2 tablespoons avocado oil or other high-heat oil  
1 teaspoon cumin seeds  
1 teaspoon coriander seeds  
1 cinnamon stick optional  
1 teaspoon fennel seeds optional  
1 dried chili pepper seeds removed  
1-2 cloves garlic minced  
Sliced green onions and/or cilantro for topping
