My brother @pengveganmunch has smashed content over the pass year and couldn’t be happier to finally do a collab with him, let us know if you make it and see your creations!

Tofu Bańh mí  
  
• 1 tbsp Soy Sauce  
• 1 ½ tsp Rice Vingear  
• 2 tsp Vegan fish sauce   
• 1/8 tbsp ginger  
• ½ clove garlic  
• 1 tbsp neutral oil  
• Add Tofu  
  
Pate  
• 180g mushrooms  
• Handful walnuts  
• ¼ tsp Ginger  
• ¼ tsp vegan fish sauce (or worcester)  
• ½ tsp soy sauce  
• Clove of garlic   
Blend in a Food Processor  
  
• Slice Cucumber  
• Salt  
• White wine vinegar  
• Sugar   
• Water   
• Carrots   
• Radish  
• Coriander
