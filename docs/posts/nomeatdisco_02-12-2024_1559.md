# NOMEATDISCO’S TOP OF THE POPS - NO. 5 - KFC MUSHROOMS 🍄  

> recipe by [@nomeatdisco](https://www.instagram.com/nomeatdisco/) 
(Sam Jones | No Meat Disco) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/DDFPQFeoA_p)

![](../img/nomeatdisco_02-12-2024_1559.png)

  
What a start to top of the pops! You guys absolutely loved this recipe and I can see why! What a banger!   
  
Ingredients:  
1 punnet of oyster mushroom clusters  
Frying oil  
Salt  
Half a cup corn flour  
Half a cup plain flour  
2 tbsp spice blend (such as Old Bay seasoning, oregano, smoked paprika, onion powder, pepper, salt)  
0.75 cup plant milk  
  
Method:  
1.Mix the wet batter ingredients (corn flour, plain flour, spice blend, plant milk) and the dry batter ingredients (corn flour, plain flour, spice blend, plant milk) in separate bowls.  
2. Coat oyster mushrooms in the wet batter, then in the dry batter until fully coated.  
3. Heat frying oil to frying temperature and fry the mushrooms until golden and crispy.  
4. Place the fried mushrooms on a drying rack, season with salt, and enjoy!
