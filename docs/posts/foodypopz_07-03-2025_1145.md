# Say "YUM" if you would eat this ONE PAN BROCCOLI CHEDDAR ORZO 🥦🧀 by  @healthygirlkitchen  

> recipe by [@foodypopz](https://www.instagram.com/foodypopz/) 
(Foody Popz) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/DG5ZyMqphsR)

![](../img/foodypopz_07-03-2025_1145.png)

  
Into a pan add:   
1/4 cup olive oil  
1 can unsweetened full fat coconut milk   
1 tsp turmeric   
1/4 cup nutritional yeast   
1 tsp garlic powder  
1 tsp dried basil  
1 tsp dried oregano  
1 tsp dried thyme  
Pepper to taste  
Juice of 1 lemon   
16 oz water   
  
Mix until combined..  
  
Then add:   
1 head chopped broccoli   
1 1/2 cups orzo   
  
(Make sure orzo Is completely covered by liquid0)  
  
Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes at 400 F. Until orzo is tender and cooked.   
  
Give it a good mix! Feel free to top with parmesan! Enjoy!
