# Combined my two favorite sweet treats to make these Banana Bread Cinnamon Rolls 🍌🤎 They’re soft and pillowy and full of cinnamon sugar and caramelized bananas. The perfect sweet treat for a cozy night in! They also make a lovely sweet breakfast served alongside a hot cup of coffee ☕️ 

> recipe by [@frommybowl](https://www.instagram.com/frommybowl/) 
(From My Bowl | by Caitlin S) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/DQ9xR0hEcnE)

![](../img/frommybowl_12-11-2025_1742.png)

  
For the Dough:  
3/4 cup soy milk  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
4 cups all purpose flour plus more for rolling  
1/4 cup granulated sugar  
3 1/2 teaspoons active yeast  
1 teaspoon cinnamon  
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt  
3/4 cup mashed ripe banana  
4 tablespoons vegan butter  
  
For the Filling:  
1/4 cup vegan butter  
1/2 packed cup dark brown sugar  
1 tablespoon ground cinnamon  
1-2 bananas  
1/2 cup walnuts or pecans  
  
For the Frosting:  
1/4 cup vegan butter  
4 ounces cream cheese  
1 cup powdered sugar  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract or vanilla paste  
2 tablespoons chopped walnuts for garnish (optional)
