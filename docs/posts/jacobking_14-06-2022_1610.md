(Jacob King) - [see original post](https://instagram.com/p/CeyrZgaKiVo)

![](../img/jacobking_14-06-2022_1610.png)

  
Have seen @littlericenoodle , @herbifoods + a few others make this and thought it looked bangin’ so wanted to try it for my self 😊   
  
Hope you enjoy, family ✌🏾   
  
👉🏾INGREDIENTS👈🏾  
-8 mini cucumbers  
-pinch of  salt  
-3 garlic cloves (minced into paste)  
-1 tbsp chilli flakes  
-2 tbsp soy sauce   
-1 tsp miso paste  
-1 tbsp peanut oil  
-2 tbsp water  
-1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds  
-2 tbsp chilli oil  
-spring onion
