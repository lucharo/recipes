# Summer soup series continuous. Every week I am inundated (positively) with a bounty of greens. This week our kenaf leaves were ready for harvest and the first thing I did was make soup. Kenaf also known as Berese in Frafra is one of many edible hibiscus plants that are edible. Hibiscus are in the mallow/malvacaea plant family which include baobab, okra, cacao and jute mallow. What I love about kenaf is that It’s a very easy mallow to enjoy. It’s not as slimy as baobab, okro or jute mallow and not as sour as hibiscus leaves. The perfect in between
